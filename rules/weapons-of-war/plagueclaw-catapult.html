Plagueclaw Catapult

Range Strength Armour Piercing Special Rules
12-72" 2(4) -3 BombardmentCumbersomeMove or ShootMultiple Wounds (D3+1)

Notes: This weapon shoots like a stone thrower, using the Bombardment special rule, a 5" blast template and the Stone Thrower Misfire table. When making a roll To Wound for this weapon, a roll of a natural 6 is always a success, even if the target's Toughness is so high that the weapon would not normally be able to wound it. The Multiple Wounds (D3+1) special rule applies only to a single model whose base lies underneath the central hole of the blast template.

This ruleset has been modified for German Comp use only. It is not legal for any other Old World comp, official or otherwise.
